FAMILY

Biodata

    I come from ordinary family. Living with simple and happy life. I live with my mother and my siblings. My mother is housewife. My father already passed away when I was children. At that time, I was 2 years old. Still children that doesn’t know anything.

    My family has been always by my side in ups and downs. They have though me how to be a better person. My mother is my strength as I can always count on her when I need a shoulder to cry on. She believes in me more than others do. She is the backbone of our family now. My siblings are my best friend on whom I can always fall back on. My family is my power that keeps pushing me to achieve a better life. I will forever be indebted to my family for all they have done to me. I cannot imagine my life without them.

    “My family is my life, and everything else comes second as far as what’s important to me.”
